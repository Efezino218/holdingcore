Our Programs

At Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation, we are dedicated to transforming lives through impactful empowerment programs. Rooted in compassion and a belief in every individual’s potential to rise above challenges when given the right opportunities and support, our initiatives uplift underserved communities and inspire hope.
P.E

Business Finance Support Workshop

Location: Lagos Community Center, Lagos, Nigeria

We provide financial assistance to startups and existing businesses, empowering entrepreneurs to establish or grow their ventures. This program helps create economic opportunities, supports sustainability, and fosters innovation in communities.

P.E

Empowerment Tools for Businesses

Location: University Hall, Abuja, Nigeria

Recognizing the importance of the right tools in driving success, we equip individuals with the resources needed to boost their businesses. From tailoring kits to farming equipment, this program ensures our beneficiaries can build a stable and thriving livelihood.

Foodstuffs Giveaway

Location: Beggars Village, Ebute-Metta

With heartfelt care, we provide food to vulnerable individuals, including the poor and the elderly. This initiative offers immediate relief from hunger and stands as a reminder that no one should feel forgotten or alone in their time of need.

Scholarships for Undergraduate Students

Location: Ajah, Lagos

We believe in the transformative power of education. Our scholarship program supports academically qualified undergraduates, enabling them to pursue their dreams and break the cycle of poverty.
