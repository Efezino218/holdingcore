Program Guidelines

At Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation, our programs are designed to uplift lives, foster empowerment, and create lasting change in communities. To ensure transparency, fairness, and the effective implementation of our initiatives, we have established the following guidelines:

Eligibility Criteria

- Each program has specific eligibility requirements tailored to its goals. Beneficiaries must meet these criteria to be considered.
- Applicants must provide accurate and verifiable information during registration or application.

ID Verification

- Beneficiaries are required to present a valid form of identification that matches the information provided on their application or profile. This ensures fairness and prevents fraudulent claims.

One Program Per Beneficiary Per Cycle

- To allow broader participation, beneficiaries are eligible for one program at a time during each cycle.

Transparency and Honesty

- All participants must be transparent about their needs and goals. Providing false information may result in disqualification or exclusion from current and future programs.

Respect and Decorum

- Beneficiaries and participants are expected to engage respectfully with Holdingcore staff, volunteers, and fellow beneficiaries. - Decency is required in all communications and interactions related to our programs.

Timely Participation

- Beneficiaries must attend scheduled program activities, training sessions, or distribution events on time. Failure to participate as agreed may lead to forfeiture of benefits.

Non-Transferable Benefits

- Benefits, tools, or resources provided through our programs cannot be sold, transferred, or used for purposes outside the intended goals of the program.

Loyalty and Community Alignment

- Beneficiaries must support the foundation’s values and initiatives. Actions that contradict or undermine the foundation’s mission may lead to disqualification from current and future opportunities.

Feedback and Accountability

- Beneficiaries are encouraged to provide honest feedback about the program to help us improve.
- Accountability is key. Beneficiaries must uphold the values of integrity and fairness when utilizing resources provided by the foundation.

Program-Specific Guidelines

- Each program may have additional rules or criteria specific to its objectives (e.g., business grants, scholarships, or giveaways). These will be communicated during the application process.
