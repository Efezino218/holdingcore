Meet the Founders

Sir Hope Joseph and family is the driving force behind the Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation. Their passion for empowering vulnerable individuals and families is rooted in a profound belief in the transformative power of empowerment.

Sir Hope and his family’s journey into establishing a nonprofit, charitable organization began with a heartfelt recognition of the challenges faced by many students and families, trapped in the cycle of economic hardship. It was this realization that ignited the desire to be a catalyst for change.

Our founder's dedication is more than a role; it's a calling. Their philanthropic and unwavering commitment have set the tone for the foundation's mission and impact. Sir Hope Jhs and family’s vision has today inspired a team of individual volunteers who share his passion for creating a brighter and more equitable world.

Goodwill Message from the Founder

Dear Friends and Supporters,

I am thrilled to share with you the incredible journey of Holdingcore NGO Foundation, a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering the innermost portion of the world. As the founder and CEO, I am humbled by the opportunity to make a positive impact in the lives of individuals and communities.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves access to resources, support, and opportunities to thrive. We pride ourselves on empowering vulnerable individuals and families, regardless of their background, tribe, religion, creed, or status. Our commitment to inclusivity and diversity is unwavering, and we strive to create a world where everyone has the chance to reach their full potential.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound realization: that even small acts of kindness can have a transformative impact. As the saying goes, *"If you can't feed a hundred people, feed just one."* This philosophy has guided our efforts, and we are committed to spreading goodwill and making a difference in the lives of those we serve.

I am grateful for the support of my family, who have been the driving force behind Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation. Together, we have created a platform that inspires hope, fosters resilience, and empowers individuals to reach their full potential. My family's dedication to philanthropy and community service has been a source of inspiration for me, and I am proud to carry on this legacy...

As we celebrate our milestones and achievements, we remain committed to our mission. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive, and we will continue to work tirelessly to make that vision a reality. Our programs and initiatives are designed to address the unique needs of our beneficiaries, and we are constantly seeking new and innovative ways to make a positive impact.

Some of our most recent achievements include:

  • Awarding a ₦5 million business finance support grant to Mrs. Emmanuella.
  • Presenting a 2010 Toyota Camry worth ₦10 million to Mr. Stephen.
  • Providing a ₦3 million business finance support grant to Miss Deborah.

This achievement is a testament to their hard work, dedication, and innovative spirit. We are proud to have played a role in supporting their business endeavors and look forward to seeing the impact they will make in their community respectively.

The grant award ceremony, which took place in Kaduna, was a momentous occasion that brought together stakeholders from various sectors. The event was a testament to the power of collaboration and community engagement. We are grateful for the support of everyone who avail themselves to be part of the event, who share our vision of creating a brighter future for all.

As we look to the future, we are excited to expand our programs and initiatives to reach even more individuals and communities. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to thrive, and we are committed to making that vision a reality.

Our strategic plan for the next ten years includes:

  • Expanding our economic empowerment programs to reach more women and youth
  • Launching a new initiative focused on education and skills development
  • Strengthening our partnerships with local organizations and stakeholders
  • Increasing our advocacy efforts to promote policy changes that support the most vulnerable members of society

We invite you to join us on this journey. Together, we can create a brighter, more equitable world for all.

Sincerely,
Sir. Hope Hopeworth
Founder and CEO, Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation.

Our Origins: A Vision of Hope

Our journey began with a clear vision and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of empowerment. We recognized that vulnerable children and families faced formidable challenges, often trapped in a cycle of economic hardship. The need for change was undeniable, and Holdingcore Empowerment Foundation was born out of a desire to be that catalyst for transformation.

Our Coverage: Making a Global Impact

We extend our assistance to people and families of Africa, Latin America, and North America, and the world at large. Our commitment to empowerment knows no borders, and we are dedicated to uplifting lives in diverse communities across the globe.

Our Approach: The Power of Empowerment

We advocate the power of empowerment. Our approach is to equip individuals with the tools they need to create sustainable livelihoods. This includes access to education, training, and financial resources, as well as a nurturing environment that encourages entrepreneurship and innovation. We understand that personal empowerment leads to collective progress.

Our Impact: Transforming Lives and Communities

Through our comprehensive programs and unwavering dedication, we have witnessed lives transformed, communities uplifted, and futures built. The impact of our work is seen in the stories of individuals who, with our support, have broken free from the shackles of poverty, established businesses, and secured brighter futures. We believe that empowerment is a path to lasting change, and our commitment to this path remains steadfast.
